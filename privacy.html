Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you choose to save your teaching style assessment results:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your institution (optional)
  • Your teaching style assessment results
  • The date and time of your assessment

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To save your assessment results for your future reference
  • To generate personalized teaching style reports
  • To conduct anonymous research on teaching styles and preferences
  • To improve the assessment tool and recommendations

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in a Firebase database. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information

To exercise these rights, please contact us at [your-email@example.com].

Anonymous Usage

You can use the teaching style assessment without providing personal information. In this case, we only collect anonymous assessment results for research purposes.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
